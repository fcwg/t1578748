Programs

 Ranked Juniors

Designed for top players with a minimum USTA Norcal ranking of 100 their respective age groups. An intense mentally and physically challenging program. Try-out required before enrollment.

 Training Program II

This program is designed for the junior competing at the high school and/or USTA level. Program is geared toward using the fundamentals to develop strategy and court positioning.

Training Program III

This program is designed for the junior who has played for more than one year. This program will introduce grips, different spins, footwork, and balance. Intensity of fitness program also increases.

Tournament Training II

This program is geared toward the younger junior training year-round, practicing five days per week, and competing in the USTA 10's and 12's age divisions. This is an invite only program and a try-out is required prior to

High Performance III

Designed for younger juniors training year-round, practicing five days per week, beginning to compete in USTA Novice and Challenger tournaments. This is an invite only program and a try-out is required prior to registration.

Junior Masters

This program is a transition program into Training Program III. Players will be introduced to a larger court and will begin learning the different types of spins as well as learning how to rally with low-pressure balls.

Junior Aces

This program is designed for the entry-level player with little or no prior tennis experience. Students will be introduced to the fundamentals of tennis. In addition, the program focuses on developing balance, footwork, hand-eye coordination, and wrist control.

Future Slammers

This program is designed for the younger junior and focuses on developing the basic fundamentals of the game in addition to work on focus as well as hand-eye coordination.

 Adult Programs

We have a growing number of drop-in clinics that we run at ClubSport San Ramon and Livermore Valley Tennis Club. Please check the individual pages for our weekly schedule of clinics.
